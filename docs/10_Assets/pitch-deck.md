Slide 2 — Problem

❗ Digital creators cannot safely license their IP.

Creators’ Pain (global)
	•	Licensing happens via DM / email / PDF
	•	No standard pricing or usage terms
	•	High risk of misuse / infringement
	•	Cross-border deals = very difficult
	•	No rights logs = no protection

Companies’ Pain
	•	Don’t know who owns the IP
	•	Unclear usage rules
	•	Slow negotiations
	•	Legal risk → stopped campaigns

Conclusion:

“IP licensing is a $100B+ global market running on broken workflows.”

Visual Guide:
	•	2分割図（Creator Pain / Company Pain）
	•	DM chaos → risk → lost revenue

Companies can:

	•	Get clear usage terms
	•	Reduce legal risk

IP Connect = Licensing × Workflow × Rights Log
